# Orbbec Gemini 2L Camera

The Orbbec Gemini 2 L is an active stereo IR camera capable of delivering high-quality depth data in various lighting conditions, suitable for 3D sensing and robotics.

## Technical Specifications

| Feature | Specification |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Depth Tech** | Active Stereo IR |
| **Resolution** | Depth: up to 1280x800 @ 30fps <br> RGB: up to 3840x2160 (4K) @ 30fps |
| **Depth Range** | 0.2m to 10m |
