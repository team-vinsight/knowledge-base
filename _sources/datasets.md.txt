# Datasets

## Visual SLAM Datasets
*   **EuRoC MAV Dataset**: [Link](http://projects.asl.ethz.ch/datasets/doku.php?id=kmavvisualinertialdatasets)
    *   Visual-Inertial datasets collected on board a Micro Aerial Vehicle (MAV).
*   **TUM VI Dataset**: [Link](https://vision.in.tum.de/data/datasets/visual-inertial-dataset)
    *   Visual-Inertial dataset for evaluating VIO/SLAM.
*   **KITTI Vision Benchmark Suite**: [Link](http://www.cvlibs.net/datasets/kitti/)
    *   Foundational dataset for autonomous driving with stereo, LiDAR, and various benchmarks.
*   **TartanAir Dataset**: [Link](https://theairlab.org/tartanair-dataset/)
    *   Large-scale photorealistic simulation dataset with challenging environments (lighting, weather, dynamic objects).
*   **TUM RGB-D Benchmark**: [Link](https://vision.in.tum.de/data/datasets/rgbd-dataset)
    *   Benchmark containing RGB-D data sequences with ground truth for evaluating RGB-D SLAM.
