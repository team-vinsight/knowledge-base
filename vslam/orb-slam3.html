Research & Related Work

Project: UAV-assisted Visual SLAM Generating Reconstructed 3D Scene Graphs in GPS-denied Environments

This work showcases map reconstruction of indoor environments to generate 3D scene graphs using a camera mounted on a drone. An aerial robot equipped with a companion computer and an RGB-D camera was integrated with a Visual SLAM framework. Structural elements like doors and walls were labeled with printed fiducial markers.

  • Pre-print: arxiv.org/abs/2402.07537

  • Underlying Framework: arxiv.org/abs/2309.10461
