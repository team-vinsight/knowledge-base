Orbbec Gemini 2L Camera

The Orbbec Gemini 2 L is an active stereo IR camera capable of delivering high-quality depth data in various lighting conditions, suitable for 3D sensing and robotics.

Technical Specifications

Feature

Specification

Depth Tech

Active Stereo IR

Resolution

Depth: up to 1280x800 @ 30fps
RGB: up to 3840x2160 (4K) @ 30fps

Depth Range

0.2m to 10m

Field of View (FOV)

Depth: H91° V66°
RGB: H86° V55°

IMU

Built-in 6-axis IMU

Connectivity

USB 3.0 Type-C
